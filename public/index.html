Step 2

Signal Strategy

What makes a founder-led B2B company worth reaching out to right now. Agent 1 uses these weights to score every prospect 0–100.

80 – 100Hot

Priority outreach this week. 2+ timing signals, strong ICP fit.

60 – 79Qualified

One clear timing signal. Outreach within 14 days.

40 – 59Watch list

Good fit, weak timing. Re-check in 30 days.

0 – 39Archive

Not the right moment. Re-surface in 90 days or skip.

Signal Scoring Rubric

Base score: 30 (solid ICP fit). Add or subtract below.
Signal Weight Priority Where to find
New plant / branch / office / project announced +25 HIGH LinkedIn post, news, company website
Hiring a sales / BD / operations leadership role +20 HIGH LinkedIn Jobs, Apollo, Naukri
Founder posting about growth, delegation, systems, or hiring on LinkedIn +20 HIGH LinkedIn (founder profile, last 30 days)
New enterprise client win or marquee project announced +15 MED-HIGH LinkedIn, company website, news
PE / institutional investment received +15 MED-HIGH Tracxn, Inc42, SEBI filings, news
Industry award or recognition received +10 MEDIUM LinkedIn, news, company website
Basic tech stack (Tally, spreadsheets, WhatsApp) — signals systems gap +10 MEDIUM Apollo tech enrichment, BuiltWith
Company visibly stuck at founder-led sales (inference from size vs. team) +10 MEDIUM LinkedIn team page, Apollo headcount
Company less than 2 years old −10 DISQUALIFY LinkedIn, company website, Zaubacorp
Enterprise scale already (500+ headcount, 200+ crore) −10 DISQUALIFY Apollo, LinkedIn
No B2B component (pure retail or consumer) −15 DISQUALIFY Website, LinkedIn, Apollo
